AI Agent Help:
--------------------------------------------------
- Ask me to explain a trading or financial term.
  (e.g., "What is a stock?", "Explain RSI")
- I will check my local knowledge first, then ask the AI for more.
- The AI will provide a definition and an example, then ask if it made sense.

Available commands:
  /help        - Show this help message.
  /my_terms    - List terms I have predefined definitions for.
  /reset       - Clear the current conversation history and start fresh.
  quit or exit - Exit the agent.
--------------------------------------------------
